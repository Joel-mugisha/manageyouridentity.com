Netiquettes is a combination of two words network and etiquettes. It is describe the the rules of conduct for respectful and appropriate communication on the internet.(What Is Netiquette - Definition, Meaning and Examples)

It is also referred as etiquettes for the internet .It is often used to know how to deal with unknown people you meet on the internet .the rules of netiquettes depend on the type of the platform and the target users and the developers have the resposibility to punish those who vioalte them.

What is the importance of Netiquettes?

The netiquettes are very important especially in online communication(like forums, groups) to insure a very useful and healthy communication. they do this by requiring to the participants to:

> the respect of the purpose of the forum or the group:On social media, there are groups with different purpose like professional, friendship, forums,etc.So when writing a comment or a message , you should be mindfull of how it will be seen or appreciated by the others members of the group or forum.

respect others privacy:by avoiding taking screenshots or recording an online live without their permission

Be inclusive: During an online meeting or discussion, in case of disagreement, interrupting someone in the middle of his speech is unparliamentary. When a disagreement happens during the discussion, it is better to find a moderate way to argue on what you disagree.

Be prepared: everytime you have an online discussion(video call), you should sign up before the starting time,have note taking tools(if it is a professional meeting) and also present yourself if the meeting requires to every attendant to turn on their cameras by ckecking if the camera and teh microphone are working properly and also dressing appropriately.
